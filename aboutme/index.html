About Me

Nathan Lipovich

I'm from Washington State and I'm the oldest of 5 kids in my family.

I'm a software engineering major who loves music, films and filmmaking, video games, and trains (especially British steam engines). I play trumpet and would love to learn french horn someday. I also have a gaming channel on Youtube where I primarily create Minecraft videos, both by myself and with other Minecraft content creators I've gotten to know through my 7 1/2 years on Youtube.

One of the reasons I love making videos on Youtube is because it's a way I've found to be creative by combining my interests (in this case gaming and video making). My career goal is to do that very thing, combine my skills and interests in a way that allows me to create art through storytelling. Obviously with a degree in software engineering, video game design is a prime example of a possible career, or I would love to simply be a filmmaker with a programming background to fall back on if necessary. One of the things I dread most is to be stuck in a job that I don't find fulfilling. I want to do what I can to have a career I can enjoy and be proud of while also being able to support the family I hope to start in the coming years.

I've been a member of the Church my whole life and served my mission split half and half between Wichita, Kansas and the Vancouver, Washington Service Mission due to mental health issues. Service missions have recently been combined with traditional proselyting missions which I feel like is a great way of proving that we all have a worth and a place in the world despite any struggles we have.
